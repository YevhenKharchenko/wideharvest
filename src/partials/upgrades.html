Tools & Upgrades

Sharper blades. Faster swings. Smarter harvesting.

Progress doesn’t just make numbers go up — it changes how the harvesting feels. Upgrades can give you sharper blades for cleaner cuts, faster swing speed for bigger clears, and special abilities that help you push through tricky moments. The best part is choosing what kind of cutter you want to become: careful and precise, or fast and unstoppable. Each upgrade makes the next field feel a little more manageable — and a lot more satisfying. Upgrade Paths:

  • Blade sharpness (cleaner, more efficient harvest)

  • Swing speed (clear fields faster)

  • Special abilities (situational boosts for obstacles or efficiency)

  • Tool unlocks (new cutters with different strengths)
