About the Game

A crop-cutting adventure that feels good in your hands.

In Harvest It, you step into the role of a skilled crop cutter and work your way through fields packed with ripe produce.
There's a good mix of fast-paced action and longer-term goals. Pretty much everything you do helps you unlock better stuff.

The goal is straightforward: harvest efficiently, maximize your yield, and keep improving. What makes it stick is the feeling — that clean slice through crops, the visual “wave” of a field being cleared, and the steady sense that you’re getting better.
