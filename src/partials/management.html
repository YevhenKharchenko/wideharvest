Farm Management

From cutter to owner: build the farm your way.

As you progress, Harvest It opens up the bigger picture — your own farm operation. You’ll make decisions about what to plant, how to organize your layout, and where to invest for maximum efficiency. It’s a light strategy layer that makes your progress feel personal, like you’re not just completing levels but growing something. When your choices start paying off, the game shifts from “harvest a field” to “run the whole system.” What You Manage:

  • Which crops to plant for profit and pacing

  • Layout choices for smoother harvesting routes

  • Investments into machinery and equipment

  • Expansion decisions to grow your operation’s reach
