How to Play

Looks simple at first, but man, once you're juggling customers and upgrades and everything... it gets complicated fast.

  • 1

    Inspect incoming devices to identify issues

  • 2

    Perform repairs like screen replacement, battery swaps, cleaning and diagnostics

  • 3

    Earn currency for successful repairs

  • 4

    Upgrade tools, unlock new parts and expand the shop

  • 5

    Serve customers efficiently to grow reputation and revenue
