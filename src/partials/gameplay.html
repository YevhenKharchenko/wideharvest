How It Plays

Swipe. Slice. Time it right.

You harvest by swiping through crops with your cutter, focusing on precision and timing. Some fields reward careful, controlled movement, while others push you into faster, bolder cuts. Once you start reading the fields better, you can grab way more crops without wasting moves. Once you figure it out, you get into this loop - harvest, collect stuff, upgrade, do it again. Yeah, it's addictive as hell.

  • Swipe to cut through ripe crops

  • Plan your route to maximize yield

  • Adjust speed and angle for different fields

  • Collect rewards after each harvest
